Overview

A complete visual identity and component library for a growing SaaS platform.

The company had scaled from 5 to 60 people in two years, but their visual identity never caught up. The product used 4 different button styles, 3 color palettes, and a logo that dated back to 2018. We needed to start fresh — with a system that could scale with the team.

Process

Audit → Strategy → Identity → Token system → Component library.

We started with a full audit of existing touchpoints: product UI, marketing site, pitch decks, and email templates. Then defined brand values and visual direction through workshops with the founding team. The result was a tight identity: a custom wordmark, a 5-color palette with 48 tokens, and an icon set of 80 custom glyphs.

Design System

120 components. One source of truth. Used by 3 product teams.

The component library was built in Figma with auto-layout, variables, and full documentation. Every component has 3–5 states, dark mode variants, and accessibility annotations. The system is now maintained by an internal design team and updated on a 6-week cycle.
