Overview

Redesigning a complex analytics product used by 10,000+ finance professionals daily.

The existing dashboard had accumulated years of features without a coherent structure. Users were spending too much time finding information and making simple errors due to interface complexity. Our goal was to reduce cognitive load while preserving the depth of data professionals needed.

Research

Talking to users revealed the real problem wasn't the data — it was navigation.

We conducted 18 user interviews and 6 contextual observation sessions. The most common complaint: users had to click through 4–5 screens to find information they needed multiple times per day. We mapped every user flow and identified 12 high-frequency tasks that could be surfaced directly.

Solution

A context-aware layout that adapts to each user's most frequent actions.

We redesigned the information architecture from scratch — moving from a tab-based structure to a customisable widget dashboard. Each user's top 5 actions are pinned to the command bar. Secondary data is accessible within 2 clicks.

We also introduced a unified data table component that replaced 8 different legacy table styles, making the interface feel consistent for the first time.
