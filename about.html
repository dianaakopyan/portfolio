A Product Designer with 9+ years of experience — specializing in transforming complex ideas into simple, intuitive, and impactful user experiences. My background is rooted in analytics, planning, and complex systems.

How I work

Curious by nature, I dive deep before designing. I ask questions, challenge assumptions, and get obsessed with the "why" behind every feature.

My process is part logic, part intuition — and a lot of collaboration. I generate and test ideas quickly in uncertain and early-stage environments. I take full ownership and move projects forward without constant supervision. I organize processes and teams to deliver clear, tangible results.

From problem to research, design, and validation — I guide projects end-to-end.

What I value

Honesty in design. Empathy in communication. Clarity in thinking.

I believe good design happens when everyone at the table feels heard and when every decision has a reason behind it. It's why I focus on products where thinking and direction matter as much as execution.

Where I fit

Where my strengths match your needs

  • Early-stage and complex products where exploration matters
  • Teams that value ownership and fast decision-making
  • Projects needing structure, not just screens

Where my approach may not fit

  • Screens executed exactly as predefined, no room for exploration
  • Focus on visual decoration rather than product thinking
  • Slow, bureaucratic environments resistant to fast iteration
Outside of work

I find inspiration in psychology, architecture, and quiet observation. I love systems — whether in design, interiors, or life itself.

I believe beauty starts with how things work, not how they look.
